# TheReplyte Pricing Model

## Target market

Small and medium businesses in Sri Lanka who use WhatsApp for customer service:
- Retail shops
- Restaurants
- Hotels / guest houses
- Clinics / salons
- E-commerce sellers
- Professional service providers

## Annual discount

Offer 2 months free for annual payment:
- Starter annual: LKR 120,000 (save LKR 24,000)
- Growth annual: LKR 250,000 (save LKR 50,000)
- Business annual: LKR 550,000 (save LKR 110,000)

## Commission / reseller model

For agencies or resellers:
- 20% recurring commission
- White-label option available on Growth and Business tiers

## Notes

- Conversations counted per rolling 30-day window
- AI replies within human-handled conversations don't count separately
- Setup includes: business profile, products upload, AI prompt tuning, agent training
